## Less Loyal Customers
- Germany & France
- Female
- NumOfProduct < 2
- Age 25-64
- Has Credit Card
- Student & retired customers in Germany & France

## More Loyal Customers
- Spain
- Male
- NumOfProduct >= 2
- Active member
